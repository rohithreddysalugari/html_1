ROHITH SHARMA

Talent an apparently heartening term that has followed Rohith sharma around like a shadow; even haunted him at times. It seems to be a burden that the cricketing fraternity has enforced upon him and, after more than a decade in the national setting, he has been weighed down by the label. Harsha Bhogle spoke of whispers in the domestic circuit; of coaches and scouts spotting the effortless , free-flowing stroke-play of a Mumbai teenager.maek Having cruised through cricket, with an average well above 50, he shot to the limelight when he scored a brisk unbeaten triple-hundred at the Ranji level.
